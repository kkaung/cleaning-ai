## The Challenges of Post-Construction Cleaning

Construction sites are inherently messy environments, with a myriad of materials, tools, and debris scattered throughout the workspace. From sawdust and drywall dust to paint splatters and adhesive residues, the cleaning challenges are numerous and require a tailored approach.

Traditional cleaning methods may not suffice in tackling the stubborn grime and embedded contaminants that are often left behind after a construction project. Without proper cleaning, these remnants can compromise indoor air quality, damage surfaces, and leave a lasting impression of neglect – not the ideal start for a new space.
